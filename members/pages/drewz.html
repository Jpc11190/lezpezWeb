Andrew Riley Zodrow (26 June 2004 - Present) has had a signifigant influence on The Soap Clan since his introduction circa 2019. Known for his constant shenanagians and tendency to commit miniscule amounts of trolling.


LezPez/Soap Clan Contributions

Drew has been present at many LezPez events and has been a catalyst for many of them. Drew can be attributed as the inside man to both The Raid on Juanita's Taco Shop and The Raid on High School Esports. Drew is also known for bringing both prosperity as well as demise to most Minecraft servers he is a part of; being the creator of Drew Bucks, holder of The Death Sticks, and responsible for the multiple Bombings of McPort.


Academic Achevivments

Drew is well know for using his presonality to manipulate and annoy teachers into granting him positive grades for his academics. Zodrow can be attributed to the Poisoning of Mr. John Yongue through the use of toxic banana spray, a decline in Mr. Jason Felt's mental health, as well as being a member of Fred Friedman's class.
